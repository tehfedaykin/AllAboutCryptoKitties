Step 7Check gas prices before making your transaction!

Eth Gas Station

Gas prices can vary - within a few days of Cryptokitties launching gas got as high as 60 gwei - often more than the cost of a cryptokitty! You'll save money by waiting for gas prices to be low before making your transaction. (2-3 is about as good as it gets!)

WTF is this gas nonsense?

'Gas' is a shorthand used to describe the cost of powering a transaction or contract in Ethereum, which is the blockchain network that CryptoKitties is built on.

Because blockchain is decentralized, every transaction is distributed through multiple computers, not a central server. This ensures each token – in this case, each CryptoKitty – is secure and one-of-a-kind. However, it also takes more computational power, which is covered by the cost of gas. Ethereum typically communicates this cost in gwei (1 billion gwei = 1 ether/ETH).
